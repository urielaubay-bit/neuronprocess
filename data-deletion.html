Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Neuron Process operates conversational messaging assistants on behalf of businesses across Facebook Messenger, Instagram, and WhatsApp. If you have interacted with one of these assistants, or otherwise shared information with us, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data. This page explains how.

Quick request: Send an email to contact@neuronprocess.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and we will process it within 30 days.

What to Include in Your Request

To help us locate and remove your data, please include:

  • The platform you used (Facebook, Instagram, or WhatsApp).
  • The name or page of the business you were messaging.
  • The phone number or account name you used to message (so we can match your records).

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email contact@neuronprocess.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request", including the details above.
  2. We will verify the request to protect against unauthorized deletion.
  3. We will delete your associated conversation history and personal data from our systems.
  4. We will confirm completion by email.

What Gets Deleted

Upon a verified request, we delete the personal data we hold about you, including your stored conversation history and any identifiers (such as your phone number or platform user ID) associated with it. Some records may be retained where required by law (for example, to meet accounting or legal obligations); in those cases the data is restricted from further use.

Timeframe

We process verified deletion requests within 30 days and will notify you once completed.
