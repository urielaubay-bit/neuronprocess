Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Neuron Process ("Neuron Process", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to our website at www.neuronprocess.com and to the conversational messaging services we operate on behalf of our business clients, including integrations with Meta platforms (Facebook Messenger, Instagram, and WhatsApp).

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact and identity data you provide directly — such as name, email, phone number, and company — when you fill out a form or contact us.
  • Messaging content — the messages, attachments, and related metadata exchanged when you contact a business that uses our conversational assistant on Facebook, Instagram, or WhatsApp (for example, your message text, the timestamp, and your platform user or phone identifier).
  • Usage data — basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited, collected through cookies and analytics on our website.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate the conversational assistant and respond to your inquiries on behalf of the business you contacted.
  • To route conversations between automated responses and the business's human staff.
  • To provide, maintain, secure, and improve our services.
  • To communicate with you about your requests and, where permitted, our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only: (a) with the business client whose account you contacted, so they can serve you; (b) with service providers who process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations (for example, cloud hosting on Amazon Web Services and the messaging platforms operated by Meta); and (c) where required by law or to protect rights and safety.

4. Meta Platform Data

When you message a business through Facebook, Instagram, or WhatsApp, your messages are transmitted via Meta's platforms in accordance with Meta's own terms and policies. We access this data only to deliver the messaging service and handle it consistent with the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies. We retain message data only as long as needed to provide the service or as required by law.

5. Data Retention

We keep conversation data for a limited period necessary to provide continuity of service and then delete or anonymize it. You may request earlier deletion of your data at any time — see our Data Deletion Instructions.

6. Security

Our infrastructure runs on Amazon Web Services with industry-standard safeguards, including encryption in transit, access controls, and credential isolation. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information using reasonable measures.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults and businesses. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page and update the "Last updated" date above.
