About Us

We are a process automation and AI specialist firm based in Querétaro, Mexico — partnering with organizations across Latin America and beyond.

NeuronProcess was built around one conviction: operational excellence is not a destination, it’s a discipline. We combine deep expertise in business process management with hands-on experience in the latest generation of AI agents — so we can help your organization at any point in the automation journey, from the first process conversation to a fully autonomous AI-driven operation.

Our background spans BPM, RPA, iPaaS integration, decision management, and agentic AI. We are practitioners, not resellers — we implement alongside your team and stay accountable for the results.

  • We baseline your operations with a structured Process Maturity Assessment before recommending any solution.
  • We design the right solution together using the Neuron Method — an AI-native evolution of Design Thinking built for process contexts.
  • We deliver iteratively through structured Playback checkpoints, so stakeholders validate every stage before the next begins.
  • We automate with the right tool for each context — from iPaaS and BPM platforms to fully autonomous AI Agentic Systems.

Our commitment is to deliver measurable, lasting value — not just working software. We measure what matters before we start and track it after we ship, so the ROI is visible and auditable from day one.

If you can't measure it, you can't improve it

Peter Drucker's principle is at the core of everything we do. We baseline before we build, and we measure after we ship — so the impact of every automation is visible and auditable.

01We start with your process maturity — not a sales pitch

Our Maturity Assessment gives you an honest baseline of where your processes stand today: where value is leaking, which steps are candidates for automation, and what it would take to reach the next level. We never recommend a solution without first understanding the problem.

02We design the right solution before writing a single line of code

The Neuron Method workshop produces a validated TO-BE process blueprint — with every step assigned to a human, an automation, or an AI agent. Then the Playbacks Methodology structures delivery into four business-led checkpoints, so misalignments surface early, not at go-live.

03We automate intelligently — and evolve toward AI agents as you're ready

We implement across the full automation spectrum — from simple integration flows to complex long-running orchestration — and when your processes are mature enough, we extend them with AI Agentic Systems that reason, decide, and act autonomously. One partner for the whole journey.
